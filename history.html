The history behind.
A little about us:

We're just a group of friends that really enjoy old multi-player games, and came together to create a community focused on that!

Our main goal, as a community and entity, is to host classic game servers and gamemodes that everyone liked, and that in our times are decaying or lacking of hosted servers to play on, yet, this didn't start from one day to another.

The idea of this community all started one day, 5 years ago, in a LAN party, where Jawiscript was playing one of his favorite old school games "Dark Messiah", he and Frenzoid talked about how sad it was that some games that we enjoyed a lot, and had multiplayed, didn't had any servers to play on, after that, the idea wasn't brought back until a few years in the future, in a teamspeak3 call where we talked about it more thoroughly, we talked and tought how nice it could be to make a community dedicated to hosting old school game servers, yet, at that time we didn't had the means to start such project.

A few years later after that, a TF2 FF2 player called Nezzi was getting tired of the servers they were playing in, the server in specific had bad admins, and oftenly abused their powers. After a time, Nezzi and Frenzoid met, and started planning to host their own TF2 FF2 server, where they could not only have the same experience, but also improve it from what they learned in the previous servers.

After a few weeks of research and VPS tinkering, our first server was up! And thus gave the start to this community, OldBox.

And from there on, we just keept growing, trying to host game servers that are not only considered classic by the standards of today, but also that would have others enjoy their gameplay.

Hosting, as for today, requires quite a monetary expense, and the number of servers that we host are quite limtied because of this, we want to make sure that if we are hosting a server people will actually play on it, but we strive for the future, to one day have many members and thus, make the expense worth!
